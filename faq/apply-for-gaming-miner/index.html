Apply for Gaming Miner

IMPORTANT NOTICE

Arkreen Network is now running the testnet. The network behavior is subjected to change during the testing.

Q1: How can I apply for a Gaming Miner?​

Preparation​

Arkreen Network only supports applying for a Gaming Miner in a computer browser so far. In addition, you should install Metamask extension within the browser. Metamask is a crypto wallet manager. Most popular browsers such as Chrome, Edge and Firefox support Metamask extension. Visit the Extension menu (sometimes in a sub-menu of Settings) to search for and install the Metamask.

Because Arkreen Network deploys its smart contracts on Polygon, you should also have some MATIC (the native token of Polygon) to pay for the Polygon gas fee. If you already have such account, import it into Metamask. Otherwise you may create a new account in Metamask and buy some MATIC to your account before apply for Gaming Miner.

Apply for a Gaming Miner​

Follow these steps to apply for a Gaming Miner:

1. Visit the Arkreen Console from within your browser.

The console page will guide you to a login page for first-time visitors, as shown below:

If a message appears prompting to download Metamask, you should check if you have installed the Metamask extension in the browser you are using.

2. Login with Metamask.

Click Login Wallet with Metamask. A My Wallet dialog will pop up. Simply click the Popular button to call Metamask.

Enter you password to unlock Metamask.

3. Switch to Polygon mainnet.

Upon login with Metamask, if it prompts for network switch, follow the instructions given by Metamask to switch to Polygon mainnet (also known as MATIC mainnet).

This only occurs if you previously selected a blockchain network other than Polygon. You can also manually select the network in Metamask at any time.

A welcome page will appear for the first time. Accept and sign it to proceed. Note that this will not charge gas fee.

4. Connect with Metamask

Follow Metamask's instructions to connect to the account.

If you have multiple accounts in Metamask, select the one that you have MATIC in. Then, click the Connect button to login the Arkreen Console.

5. Request for a Gaming Miner

Upon login, click the Wallet icon at the upper right corner to call the My Wallet dialog. Then click Request a GamingMiner to apply for a Gaming Miner.

6. Fill in registration information and wait for Gaming Miner allocation.

Fill in your email and click Get Auth Code. Metamask will be called to sign the request. Note that this will not charge gas fee.

An email will soon be sent to you. Please copy the authenticate code in the email to the Authentication Code box and click Request to allocate a Gaming Miner for you.

Should you not receive the email after a long time, please check your spam or junk mail box.

7. Claim your Gaming Miner

An allocated Gaming Miner does not start mining until you successfully claim it. Click Claim to claim your Gaming Miner.

An NFT bound to the miner will be minted upon claiming. Metamask will be called to mint the NFT. Make sure you have enough MATIC to pay for the gas fee. Note the gas fee varies from time to time.

Claiming will typically take about 30~120 seconds (depending on network). Please stay on the page. Should you accidentally close or refresh the browser, please go back to 5. Request for a Gaming Miner and try again.

Once the Gaming Miner is successfully claimed, the page will automatically jump to you miner list.

8. Wait for rewards

Gaming Miner will start to mine automatically once it's claimed successfully. It will simulate PoGG reports every hour (12 samples per report). You can click Miners Tab and then the miner's address to view the detailed information.

Each Gaming Miner has a chance to earn a reward roughly once every 60 hours, so after a few hours you will receive the gAKRE that Gaming Miner dug up.

Withdrawal​

The reward mined by the Gaming Miner is kept by the Arkreen Network. You can withdraw the reward to transfer the gAKRE to your Polygon account.

Click the Wallet icon at the upper right corner to call the My Wallet dialog. Then click Withdraw Mining Reward button to withdraw mined reward. Withdrawal will charge gas fee.

Q2: Can I apply for more than one Gaming Miners?​

You can apply for up to one Gaming Miner with a single Polygon account.

Q3: I applied for the Gaming Miner, but did not receive a follow-up email.​

Please check if the email has gone into a spam folder or trash.

Q4: I received the email, but failed to claim the Gaming Miner due to unsuccessful NFT minting.​

It's mostly due to a lack of MATIC stored in your active Polygon account. Please check to make sure enough MATIC is available to pay the gas fee.

Q5: I claimed the Gaming Miner successfully but the Miner Status is still Registered rather than Claimed.​

Please follow the instructions in the email to claim once more. No matter how many times you try to claim the same Gaming Miner, you will be charged only once for the transaction gas fee.
