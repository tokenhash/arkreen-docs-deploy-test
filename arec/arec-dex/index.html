How to Sell/Buy ART within Arkreen DEX

Within Arkreen DEX, you could sell ART, the liquidized AREC, into other crypto tokens, such as USDC or MATIC. And as a climate actor, you are also able to buy some ART tokens within the DEX, and then burn these ART to offset your carbon footprint.

Arkreen DEX is an AMM-like decentralized exchange with zero commission fees.

Sell ART​

It is easy to swap ART for some other crypto tokens.

  • Step 1. Visit Swap page

    • On PC: Click the Exchange item in the left navigation bar.

    • On smartphone: Select Exchange item under the AREC top menu

  • Step 2. Select ART as the token you want to sell by clicking the button Select a token.

  • Step 3. Select the token you want to obtain.

    info

    Only USDC or MATIC are supported at this time.

  • Step 4. clicking the button Swap to complete the transaction.

Swap on PC
Swap on phone

Buy ART​

It is very similar to selling ART. You need to select the USDC/MATIC as the token to sell, and select the ART as the token to get.

After you buy some ART tokens, you could burn them to offset your carbon footprint and mint your retirement badge.

info

You could also buy AKRE tokens by selecting it as the token to buy.
